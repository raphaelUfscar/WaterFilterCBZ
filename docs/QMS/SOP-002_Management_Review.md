## 1. Purpose

Define how top management periodically reviews the QMS to ensure its continuing suitability,
adequacy, and effectiveness (ISO 13485:2016 §5.6).

## 2. Scope

Applies to the WaterFilterCBZ QMS as a whole.

## 3. Frequency

At planned intervals, **at least annually**, and additionally after significant change (e.g.
intended-use change, major defect, regulatory change). The interval shall be recorded.

## 4. Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Top Management | Chairs the review; makes decisions; allocates resources. |
| Quality Manager | Prepares inputs, records minutes and actions, tracks closure. |
| Engineering Lead / Risk Manager | Provide design, verification, and risk inputs. |

## 5. Review inputs (§5.6.2)

At minimum: results of [internal audits](SOP-005_Internal_Audit.md); feedback/complaints;
process performance and product conformity; status of
[CAPA](SOP-003_Corrective_and_Preventive_Action.md); follow-up from prior reviews; changes that
could affect the QMS; recommendations for improvement; applicable new/changed regulatory
requirements; status of [risk management](SOP-007_Risk_Management.md) and open
[roadmap](../iec62304_class_c_roadmap.md) gaps (risk controls, QMS gaps).

## 6. Review outputs (§5.6.3)

Decisions and actions related to: improvement of the QMS and its processes; improvement of product
related to requirements; resource needs; and changes needed. Each action has an owner and due date.

## 7. Procedure

1. Quality Manager compiles inputs (§5) into a review pack.
2. Top Management conducts the review; decisions and actions are recorded.
3. Minutes are issued as a record and approved; actions are tracked to closure and revisited at the
   next review.

## 8. Records produced

- Management review minutes (input pack, attendance, decisions, actions, due dates) — retained per
  [SOP-001](SOP-001_Document_and_Record_Control.md).

## 9. References
