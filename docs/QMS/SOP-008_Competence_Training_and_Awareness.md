## 1. Purpose

Ensure personnel performing work affecting product quality are competent on the basis of
education, training, skills, and experience, per ISO 13485:2016 §6.2.

## 2. Scope

All roles defined in the QMS (Quality Manager, Engineering Lead, Risk Manager, reviewers, auditors,
Top Management).

## 3. Procedure

1. **Define competence requirements** per role (e.g. software life-cycle, IEC 62304, ISO 14971,
   the QMS procedures).
2. **Assess** personnel against requirements; identify gaps.
3. **Provide training** or other actions to close gaps; evaluate effectiveness.
4. **Ensure awareness** of the relevance of activities and how they contribute to quality and
   safety objectives.
5. **Maintain records** of education, training, skills, and experience.

## 4. Role → competence map (template)

| Role | Required competence (examples) |
|---|---|
| Engineering Lead | Software architecture, IEC 62304 life-cycle, secure coding. |
| Risk Manager | ISO 14971 risk management. |

## 5. Records produced

- Competence requirements per role; training plans and records; effectiveness evaluations —
  retained per [SOP-001](SOP-001_Document_and_Record_Control.md).
