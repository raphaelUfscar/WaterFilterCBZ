## 1. Purpose

Define how internal audits verify that the QMS conforms to ISO 13485, to the manufacturer's own
QMS requirements, and is effectively implemented and maintained (ISO 13485:2016 §8.2.4).

## 2. Scope

All QMS processes and the design documentation set ([docs/IEC/](../IEC/)).

## 3. Principles

- **Independence:** auditors shall not audit their own work.
- **Planning:** audits are planned on a programme considering the status and importance of
  processes and prior audit results; safety-relevant areas are audited with priority.
- **Frequency:** at planned intervals, at least annually.

## 4. Procedure

1. **Plan** the audit programme and individual audit scope/criteria.
2. **Conduct** the audit against criteria; gather objective evidence.
3. **Record findings** (conformities, nonconformities, observations).
4. **Report** to responsible management; nonconformities feed
   [CAPA](SOP-003_Corrective_and_Preventive_Action.md).
5. **Follow up** to verify and record corrections; report status to
   [management review](SOP-002_Management_Review.md).

## 5. Records produced

- Audit programme; audit plans; audit reports with findings; follow-up records — retained per
  [SOP-001](SOP-001_Document_and_Record_Control.md).

## 6. References
